About New York Interior Design

We are an interior staging and design studio that creates compelling experiences for real estate and new development clients throughout New York City. We take our cue from the best of Danish Mid-Century design and translate it for the contemporary tastes of today’s savvy real estate buyers. The team consists of artists, interior stylists, set designers, marketing and business professionals all working to deliver, on trend designs, personalized service, and seamless executions.
